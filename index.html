About Me:

So how did I get into software engineering and web development? Well, honestly, it started with curiosity and wanting to know more. I had heard and seen a lot of information about coding which I thought was cool, and I wanted to explore it for myself.


I started off with some YouTube tutorials and was simply intrigued by what could be done with just lines of code. I loved the enormous room for creativity and also saw how software could be used to create solutions to everyday problems. It seemed fun and cool enough that I wanted to build my own things.


In Fall of 2022, I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp to develop my skills and make a career change. Though my microbiology background is very different than that of computer science, I think at the heart of it all, I liked finding out how things worked, building things, and solving problems.
